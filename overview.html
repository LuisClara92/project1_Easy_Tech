Your new desktop computer

You'll recognise the price along with the basic shape and size, so you can simply drop your new Easy Tech into your old projects for an upgrade; and as always, we've kept all our software backwards-compatible, so what you create on a Easy Tech will work on any older models you own too.
The speed and performance of the Easy Tech is a step up from earlier models. For the first time, we've built a complete desktop experience. Whether you're editing documents, browsing the web with a bunch of tabs open, juggling spreadsheets or drafting a presentation, you'll find the experience smooth and very recognisable — but on a smaller, more energy-efficient and much more cost-effective machine.

Silent, energy-efficient

The fanless, energy-efficient Easy Tech runs silently and uses far less power than other computers.

Fast networking

Easy Tech 4 comes with Gigabit Ethernet, along with onboard wireless networking and Bluetooth.

USB 3

Your new Easy Tech has upgraded USB capacity: along with two USB 2 ports you'll find two USB 3 ports, which can transfer data up to ten times faster.

Your choice of RAM

We're making different variants of the Easy Tech available, depending on how much RAM you need — 2GB, 4GB, or 8GB.
